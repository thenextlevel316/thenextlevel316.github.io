Social Media Work

Outdoor Adventure Centre

At the outdoor adventure centre where I work, activities like climbing, archery, and canoeing help kids build confidence, resilience, and a sense of achievement. Through social media, I’ve been learning to tell these stories, capturing the experience from both sides: the excitement and pride of the young people taking on new challenges, and the dedication and encouragement of the instructors guiding them.

Whether it’s a kid’s first time hitting the target, a team working together to paddle across the lake, or an instructor supporting someone to reach the top of the wall, each post shares a moment of growth and connection.
